Corona virus

1. Do you have fever and/or respiratory problem?

Stay home Call your doctor and report your symptoms. Do not go to the waiting room or the emergency department. The doctor will determine by telephone whether you can stay ill at home or should go to hospital.

2.Can I still be tested?

At the moment only serious cases and care personnel are being tested.

Protect your-self and help others.
